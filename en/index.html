Welcome to the new world.
The aim of this project is to create a software package that will save humanity from many global problems of today, as well as provide new opportunities for development.

What problems are we talking about?

01

Power

Most of the people in the world are deceived by their government. The laws that politicians pass do not match the needs and desires of people. The system in which we "elect" a person from our district, and he communicates our needs to the president / parliament does not work. Corporations and powerful people lobby for their laws to protect the interests of their businesses. Politicians and civil servants look at people as a workforce, thinking only about their ratings, career and money. Even if we assume that all politicians are honest, several hundred people elected several years ago cannot physically know and embody the desires and needs of tens of millions of people today.

02

Taxes

People pay taxes, but they don't get solutions of their problems. We absolutely do not see where our money goes and how it is used. There are hundreds of ways to launder money from the treasury.

03

Corruption

Corruption exists in every country. In most countries, especially underdeveloped ones, every state structure is corrupted.

04

Information

Large media are owned by groups of people who are somehow connected with the authorities. Information is provided selectively, in the form and at the time when it is beneficial to the owners of the resource.

05

Ecology

We produce a lot of waste. We use a lot of paper and plastic.

What is a solution?

There is a solution, it is in modern technologies and data openness. We will create a completely open source software platform, the basis of which will be an open database, a digital key for each person and software robots for data analyzing and alerting of people.

Let's consider in more details:

  1

Database

Its main features are complete openness of data and the inability to change old records, but only add new ones. Openness of data in itself disarms people with bad intentions. Hackings, manipulations and other dirty deeds will become physically impossible. The inability to change old records will absolutely eliminate data forgery. After all, it is impossible to fake something that does not exist, and if something that exists cannot be changed, then it cannot be faked.

  2

Digital key

We will generate a unique digital key for each person. The digital key can be stored anywhere, but better printed on a plastic card, along with your photo. With this key, each person identifies himself when making changes to an open database. In the future, he will be able to store money, documents and any other information on the same key.

  3

Data analysis

Anyone can open the database and analyze information at any time, and open source software robots will be created to help people. Robots will monitor changes in the database and notify people. Everyone can create a robot or use an existing one. When the country's data is in an open database, robots will be able to analyze the data and give the necessary answers even before questions arise.

How will this solve our problems?

01

Power

We will be able to vote absolutely transparently for anything in real time. You click "Vote" and you see how the vote counter increases, and it is your vote that appears in the open database. We will be able to transfer part or all of the legislative power evenly into the hands of each person. We will also be able to regulate the judicial and executive power systems through direct open voting. The amount of money (the amount of capital), heredity, acquaintances (connections) will no longer determine the share of power. Politicians will no longer be needed, every person will be able to vote directly for any law or create his own law for a vote.

02

Taxes

You will get full access online to reports for each coin that you donated to the treasury. Each person equally will decide where to spend money from the treasury by voting. At any time, he will be able to check where the funds were spent. Robots will also monitor your funds and report abuse to the world.

03

Corruption

When the data is open, and the movement of money and resources is monitored by robots informing the whole world about suspicious activities, it is very difficult to steal something. The more open data exist and the less the human factor is involved, the less corruption is. With our system, there will be a very few civil servants in cities, robots will monitor their every action, and the results will be visible to the whole world. This is the only way to reduce corruption to a negligible level that was not previously available to humanity.

04

Information

On the basis of our software platform, we will create information projects owned by NOBODY, where EVERYONE can be the author. When creating a publication, the author will be able to sign it with his own key, and each person will be sure of its source. The truthfulness of the information will be determined by people, increasing or decreasing the rating of each publication. Social networks now partially perform the function of independent media, but they have drawbacks: firstly, they belong to someone, and secondly, without strict identification, any user can create bots and spread misinformation. Identification on a social network with a digital key will exclude this possibility.

05

Ecology

An incredible amount of paper and plastic will not be used after the implementation of our software products. Every year this figure will grow.

Summary

An economic boom will surely follow the solution of these problems. This is a reasonable way of human development and it is inevitable. Humanity will come to this in any case, but I want it to happen in the next 5 years, not 100. People in the whole world have suffered enough. Technology and we ourselves can help a lot. It will be a leap into a new world. And, although there is nothing brilliant in this idea, but as you know, genius is in simplicity. We will really be able to unite our efforts, colonize new planets, and do other great things. Our politicians and civil servants will stop deceiving us. In fact, deception will become technically impossible. Anyone can take our open platform and organize the work of his enterprise or government structure.

Questions

What about people who do not have a smartphone or computer?

A person only needs one sheet of paper to store his key and one terminal with mobile Internet for the entire settlement to perform an operation with an open base. This will replace the current election commissions on the votes, the people you see for the first time. This will replace ballot boxes, curtains, tons of paper and plastic, but most importantly — in real time you will see where your voice went.

What if the digital key is stolen?

Firstly, we will implement additional protection, recognition of a face, fingers, voice, eyes, SMS, 2FA, at the user's choice, and secondly, the key will be tied to the person's data and the procedure for reissuing the key in case of loss will be described.
